06 · FAQ

Six questions, answered.

Is HerBody actually halal-certified?
Yes. Halal Food Authority, GB18 / 2026. No gelatin, no alcohol carriers, no haram excipients. Halal isn't a feature for us — it's the default.
How fast will I see results?
Skin elasticity in 8 weeks (the Verisol® clinical line). Strength and energy from the creatine in 2–3 weeks. Use the Mirror in the app to see your own line — not ours.
What happens to my £1?
It leaves before profit is even calculated. It lands with the cause you chose this month in the app. Audited annually. Receipts published quarterly.
Is creatine safe for women?
Yes — extensively studied. 5g daily is the dose used in the published trials. It does not cause bloating or weight gain in women at this dose.
Can I skip a month?
Skip, pause or cancel anytime in the app. No retention tricks. We'd rather you came back than felt trapped.
What if my cause changes?
Pick a new one each month. Same eight options, your choice. The cause is hers to name — that's the whole point.
