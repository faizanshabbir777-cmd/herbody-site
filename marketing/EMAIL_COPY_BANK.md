## Review-request block — DMCC GATE
Do not draft, schedule, or A/B-test any review-request email until a verifiable review platform is live and `verification_flags.reviews_exist` is true in `product-facts.json`. No quoting customer messages in marketing, no "loved by", no star imagery. This is a banned-practice risk area (DMCC Act 2024), not a style preference.
