## Why a half-scoop week?

Two reasons, both boring and both good:

1. **Habits stick when they start small.** A half scoop takes the same 30 seconds as a full one — by day 8, the ritual already exists, and you just fill the scoop the rest of the way.
2. **A gentle runway.** Old-school creatine advice involved "loading" 20g a day. The Daily doesn't do loading — it's the studied 5g, daily — and starting at half for a week lets your routine (and your taste buds) settle in gradually. Everyone's different; we won't promise you'll feel nothing and we won't promise you'll feel something. Gentle is simply a nicer way to begin.

## The daily method (all 7 days)

1. **Half scoop — 7g.** Level it off, don't heap it.
2. **200ml cold water** in your shaker.
3. **Shake 10 seconds.** Strawberry lemonade, no added sugar.
4. **Drink it in the morning** — training days AND rest days. Rest days count double for the habit.

## Day by day

**Day 1 — Anchor it.**
Pick the thing you already do every single morning — for most of us, it's the kettle. New rule: *kettle on → scoop, shake, drink → tea brews.* The plan is not "remember a supplement"; the plan is "the kettle now has a sidekick."
*Honest note: you will feel exactly nothing today. That's normal and expected — creatine works by building up gradually over weeks of daily use. Day 1 is about the anchor, not the effect.*

**Day 2 — Same time, same spot.**
Leave the pouch and shaker where the ritual happens, visible, next to the kettle or your mug. Decision-free mornings are the goal: no cupboard, no hunting.

**Day 3 — Taste check-in.**
Three days is a fair taste trial. Too strong? Use 250ml of water. Want it colder? Ice cube. Genuinely hate it? You're covered — **30-day flavour guarantee: hate it, we refund the pouch.** No essay required.

**Day 4 — The wobble day.**
Day 4 is statistically where new habits quietly die — usually a rushed morning. Pre-empt it tonight: if tomorrow is chaotic, put 7g in the dry shaker before bed. Morning-you only has to add water.

**Day 5 — Rest-day reminder.**
If today's a rest day: the scoop still happens. Consistency — not occasion — is the whole mechanism with creatine. Same scoop on the days you train and the days you don't.

**Day 6 — Place your tokens.**
Your pouch earned you **2 charity tokens** in the HerBody app. Take two minutes with your drink and allocate them across the eight vetted causes — refuge, care and crisis support among them. 20% of our profits, audited, follows the choices you all make. Good for you. Good for her.

**Day 7 — Level up tomorrow.**
Last half-scoop morning. From tomorrow it's the full 14g — same water, same 10 seconds, same anchor. You've already done the hard part: seven mornings in a row is a ritual, not an experiment.

**Day 8 onwards — the full scoop.**
14g, 200ml, every morning. Your pouch holds 21 servings — with the half-scoop week, your first pouch stretches a few days further than the label maths.
