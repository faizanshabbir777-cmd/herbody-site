Five questions, answered.

How do you choose the partners?
A combination of regulator status (UK Charity Commission, equivalent in country), on-the-ground spend efficiency, audit history, and — for the Asian partners — willingness to sign our zero-tolerance corruption policy. We update annually.
What if a partner fails an audit?
They are replaced before the next pouch ships. We publish the finding, the response, and the replacement on the audit page. No second chances, no quiet renewals — that's the policy.
Why £1, not 1%?
Because a fixed pound per pouch is honest math she can do in her head. It removes the "but how much really?" question that ruins most cause-marketing. £1 is the floor; the 10% profit share sits on top of it.
Can I see what my pouch funded?
Yes, in the Impact view of the app. Each quarter your individual pouches are mapped to the line-by-line receipts. Your pound, named.
Why is Asia over-represented?
Because the brand was built by and for women in the South Asian diaspora — and because the audit work and partner trust we have built sits deepest there. We expect Africa and global coverage to grow over Year 2.
Can I nominate a partner?
Yes. Email pact@herbody.co with the organisation's registration details and impact data. We review on a quarterly basis, with an annual partner refresh.
