01 · The Manifesto

HerBody was made because the supplement shelf is full of small lies — marketing doses, made-for-Instagram pastel pouches, charity logos that never lead anywhere honest. And because halal, on that shelf, almost never meant clinical.

We made one pouch instead of nine. Every nutrient she'd otherwise have to buy from seven brands, at clinical dose, in a flavour she actually likes. Halal by default — not as a feature, as the floor.

Then we made it so that her care funds another woman's care. A pound and a tenth from every pouch, to one of eight partners she chooses. The pound leaves before the profit is calculated. The four Asian partners are audited annually for zero-tolerance on corruption. The receipts are published quarterly, signed.

Profit is the fuel. It is never the destination.

— The founders
London · January 2026 · Vol. 26 No. 01
02 · The Mission

Value, measured in lives.
Not in margin.

Every decision inside HerBody is asked the same question first: does this make her life, or a sister's life, materially better? If the honest answer is no, we don't do it — even when the spreadsheet says we should.

i · Promise

Clinical dose, always.

Every ingredient at the dose studied in published trials. Not "active". Not "supportive". The actual dose.

ii · Promise

Halal by default.

No gelatin, no alcohol carriers, no haram excipients. Halal Food Authority, GB18 / 2026.

iii · Promise

£1 leaves first.

Before tax, before margin, before salary. A pound per pouch lands with her cause, every pouch, no fine print.

iv · Promise

Signed receipts.

Every Asian partner audited annually by PKF Littlejohn for zero-tolerance on corruption. Quarterly ledger, public.

03 · The Build

Eighteen months before we sold.

A timeline of the work that came before the launch — formulation, audit set-up, partner due diligence, the long letter to Halal Authority.

Q3 · 2024

The thesis.

Founders meet at a kitchen table in Whitechapel. The brief is two lines: clinical doses, halal default, a pound that leaves before the profit does.

Q4 · 2024 — Q2 · 2025

The formula.

Fourteen formulation rounds. Three clinical advisors. The dose floor set at studied amounts: 5g creatine, 5g Verisol® collagen, 100% NRV multivitamin.

Q3 · 2025

The partners.

Forty-two candidates reviewed. Eight chosen. Audit standard set with PKF Littlejohn. Halal certification submitted to HFA · GB18.

Q1 · 2026

The launch.

The Daily ships from London. First Pact ledger published. Vol. 26 No. 01 of The Quarterly goes out by post to the founding 500.
