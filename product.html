What it does.

The Daily is built around two clinically-studied actives — creatine monohydrate at 5g (the dose used in the published trials for strength, energy and cognitive support), and Verisol® bioactive collagen peptides at 5g (the dose clinically shown to improve skin elasticity in 8 weeks).

Around them sits a complete multivitamin at 100% NRV, plus inositol 40:1 for hormonal balance, dandelion for liver support, cranberry PAC for urinary health, and methylated B-vitamins for energy.

What it doesn't have.

No gelatin. No alcohol carriers. No haram excipients. No stevia aftertaste, no chalkiness, no rose-pink "for-women" marketing dust. No magnesium stearate, no titanium dioxide, no synthetic colours.

And — quietly important — no proprietary blends. Every dose is on the panel, in milligrams, at the level the peer-reviewed work calls effective.

Frequently asked.

Is it actually halal-certified?

Yes. Halal Food Authority, certificate GB18 / 2026. No gelatin, no alcohol carriers, no haram excipients. Halal isn't a feature for us — it's the default. The certificate is on the audit page if you want to see it.

How fast will I see results?

The Verisol® clinical line is skin elasticity improvement in 8 weeks. Strength and energy from the creatine show up sooner, usually in 2–3 weeks. The Mirror in the app tracks your own line so you're not guessing.

Is creatine safe for women?

Extensively studied and yes. 5g daily is the standard research dose. It does not cause bloating, weight gain or "bulk" in women at this dose — that's a persistent myth from the 1990s bodybuilding era.

Can I take it during pregnancy?

Please speak to your doctor before taking any supplement in pregnancy or while breastfeeding. We don't make claims either way — your clinician should make this call with the panel in front of them.

What if I don't love the flavour?

Email hello@herbody.co within 30 days and we'll refund the pouch, no questions. We don't use retention tricks. We'd rather you came back than felt trapped.

Where does my £1 go?

To the cause you chose this month in the app — one of eight audited partners. It leaves before profit is even calculated. Receipts are published quarterly, line by line. The Asian partners (Shaukat Khanum, Edhi, Pakistan flood relief, Girls' Education) are independently audited annually with zero-tolerance on corruption.
