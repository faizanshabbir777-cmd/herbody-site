# HerBody — Customer Support SOP

## 1. Channels & service levels

| Channel | Use | Target response |
|---|---|---|
| Email — `hello@` (support inbox, launch blocker #6) | Primary. All order, product, refund, cert requests | Within 1 working day; same day before 15:00 |
| Instagram / TikTok DMs | Redirect to email for anything involving orders/accounts (no PII over DM); quick answers otherwise | Within 1 working day, checked during the morning routine |
| Contact form (`/pages/contact`) | Feeds the email inbox | as email |

No phone support at launch — stated honestly on the contact page. Escalation: anything legal (statutory-rights disputes, ASA/CMA mentions, press), medical (adverse reaction reports), or angry-after-two-replies goes to the founder same day. **Adverse reaction reports**: acknowledge with care, never argue, never diagnose; log symptoms + batch code; founder reviews; advise the customer to speak to their GP or pharmacist and to report via the MHRA Yellow Card scheme if they wish; refund without quibble.

## 2. Tone — clinical AND kind

- Answer the actual question in the first sentence. Then add warmth, not before.
- Numbers and documents beat reassurance: attach the COA, quote the dose, link the policy.
- Never defensive, never scripted-sounding; read it aloud before sending.
- Never health advice. We know our label; we are not her GP — and we say so plainly when asked anything clinical.
- British English, first person plural. Sign with a first name.

Missed days happen — just carry on the next morning. Please don't double up; stick to the stated dose. Consistency over intensity, always."

## 4. What support never does

- Never writes or solicits anything that could read as a fabricated review/testimonial, and never asks customers for reviews **at all** while the reviews programme is disabled (DMCC, Decision D6).
- Never makes health claims beyond `docs/CLAIMS_MATRIX.md` — support email is a marketing surface in the ASA's eyes.
- Never uses the flavour guarantee to deflect a statutory-rights request (`operations/RETURNS_SOP.md` interplay rule).
- Never discusses another customer's order; identity check (order number + email match) before any account detail.

## 5. Feedback loop

Weekly: tag counts (`refund_flavour_guarantee`, delivery issues, top macro usage) go into the Monday digest via the metrics agent. Three similar questions in a week = a FAQ page fix, not three more good emails.
